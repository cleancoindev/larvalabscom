Sidekick Products

Audiolab+

AudioLab+ lets you record up to 30 seconds of audio through the built in mic, then edit it, apply effects and send it to your friends. Use it to ask your friends the name of the song you're hearing, or just send fart sounds like we know you want to.

Camera Zoom

Camera Zoom puts a 6x zoom lens on your Sidekick. Also includes timer countdown and quick send for photos via email or MMS.

Kick Drum Old School

Kick Drum Old School is the vintage edition of the popular drum machine that lets you write beats on your phone. It comes with five full drum kits, including two new old school sets, and a human beat box! Sounds can be played both forward and reverse, with funky results.

ScreenPops

While your phone is locked it usually displays a photo. With ScreenPops, you can make it display a scrolling stylized message or graphic. Choose from six fonts with lots of styles, make the message as big or small as you want.

Comic Lab

Comic Lab lets you create full comic strips right on your phone. Add up to four photos, and Comic Lab changes them to look like they were drawn as a comic.

Animated Screensaver

Animated Screensaver lets you put awesome animations on the lock screen of your phone. Choose from a wide variety of animations to display, or let the phone pick a different one for you every time it goes to sleep.

Beat Lab

BeatLab is a mobile beat generator that can cut up, rework and generate beats on the fly to give you new ideas and inspiration.

Photo Twist

PhotoTwist lets you bend and warp your photos. Just pick one of the six different effects, then drag your finger to distort any part of the photo. Effects can be stacked and applied as many times as you want to any part of a photo.

Photo Lab

PhotoLab lets you apply cool effects to your photos. 12 different effects are available, including Retro, Night Vision, Multi-Color, Sepia and more. Apply one or more effects to your photos and then save the results to your gallery or send them via email.

Kick Mix

KickMix is a fully functional set of DJ turntables that allows you to DJ your MP3s right on your phone. Load two MP3 files from the memory card on your phone, and watch as the records spin and you hear both of your tracks.

Kick Synth

KickSynth is a 10 track MIDI music production studio for your phone. It includes over 100 instruments and a full drum kit. It also supports all the features you need to write complete songs - copy/paste/transpose along with shuffle mode, tempo control and dynamics.
