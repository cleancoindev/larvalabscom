iPhone Products

Cover Mosaic

Recreate any photo using the album art on your iPhone or iPod. CoverMosaic uses a color matching algorithm to replace each part of your photo with album art from your music library.

Face Mixer v2.0

On this new version: take photos of your friends or get the pictures from Facebook and combine their faces in ridiculous ways. You can share your results on Facebook with just a tap!

Retro Defense

Retro Defense is a tower defense game with a killer retro theme. Your goal is to stop the invaders from getting through the board.

Photo Twist

PhotoTwist lets you bend and warp your photos. Just pick one of the six different effects, then drag your finger to distort any part of the photo. Effects can be stacked and applied as many times as you want to any part of a photo.

WordPops

WordPops is an addictive cross between Tetris® and Scrabble®. Spell words with your finger to stop the bubbles from filling the screen. Earning more points by spelling difficult words.

Flip Clock

Flip Clock is a stylish flip style clock for your iPhone or iPod Touch. Bring it on a trip instead of a separate clock, or stand it up beside your bed or on your desk at work.

Photo Flip Clock

Photo Flip Clock lets you customize a stylish flip clock with photos you take yourself. Just take a photo, or choose one from your gallery, and make it one of the digits in your clock.

Breath Pacer

BreathPacer is a powerful breathing guide that can help you reduce stress and experience deep relaxation. Andrew Weil, a leader in the integrative health field, states, "If I had to limit my advice on healthier living to just one tip, it would be simply to learn how to breathe correctly."
