Beta Testers Needed

by Matt Hall on iPhone /

Our next iPhone app is almost beta worthy, so it’s time to put out the call for beta testers. Anyone interested can say so in a comment below. The requirements are as follows:

  1. Have OS 3.0+ on your device (can be an iPhone or iPod)
  2. Have a reasonably large music collection on your device (50+ albums minimum, 200+ ideal)
  3. Be serious about providing feedback, we are given limited beta tester slots so we don’t want to waste them

Anyone who participates will get a free version of the app when it’s launched, and if you’re really good we’ll throw in free versions of some of our other stuff too Anyone who is helpful will also probably be included in any future beta testing too, so you’ll always have the latest and greatest.

UPDATE: Anyone interested should go sign up atÂ http://ibetatest.com and then include their user name in the comment they post. We’ll use that site to manage the distribution of keys and updates while we’re in beta.

UPDATE 2: The app has been submitted to Apple for release, so we’re no longer in active testing. However, I’ll add any iBetaTest names posted below to the beta so when we do updates you can be a part of that testing.
