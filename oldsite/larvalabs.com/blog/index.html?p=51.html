WordPops

by Matt Hall on Android /

We just released WordPops for Android last night. It’s a port of our super addictive word game for iPhone and we managed to keep all the features, including:

  • A Scored game mode to keep track of your top scores achieved before you run out of space for bubbles
  • A Relaxing game mode allows you to proceed at a steady pace and go for longer words
  • 2x, 3x and rare 5x word score bubbles
  • Freeze bubbles stop new bubbles from coming to give you a break
  • Mutate bubbles change the letters of adjacent bubbles
  • Explosive bubbles destroy adjacent bubbles to clear large parts of the board
  • Challenge your friends to beat your high score over email

Check out the screenshots below, Google phone users can click here to check out the game in the market. There’s also a free version you can get here to try it out. Send us a challenge if you think you’ve got a good score!
