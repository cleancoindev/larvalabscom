New Retro Defense Maps for Android

by Matt Hall on Android /

We’ve just uploaded a new version of Retro Defense for Android, the new version is 1.1.2 and contains 4 new maps. Two of these maps were designed by people who stopped by our booth at Google IO – thanks guys! You can see their names next to the map in the game.

Android users can see the full game in the market by clicking here.

Let us know what you think of the new maps, there’s more in the works!
