Android Products

Photowall Live Wallpaper

PhotoWall shows your photos as your wallpaper! See an ever changing set of photos from Your phone's camera gallery, your own Facebook photos, your friends' Facebook photos or Facebook photos tagged as having you in them!

Androidify

Create Android versions of yourself and friends. Set as a contact photo, or post to Twitter and Facebook. Resize, recolor and choose from lots of clothing options to get your Android just right!

Created together with the Google Creative Lab, see all the details here.

Slide Screen

Larva Labs presents a home screen that creates a meaningful hierarchy out of your information.

Built for Android-based handsets, our home screen is designed for heavy phone users and people struggling with information overload.

Retro Defense

Retro Defense is a tower defense game with a killer retro theme. Your goal is to stop the invaders from getting through the board.

Photo Twist

PhotoTwist lets you bend and warp your photos. Just pick one of the six different effects, then drag your finger to distort any part of the photo. Effects can be stacked and applied as many times as you want to any part of a photo.

Battle for Mars

Battle for Mars is a huge turn based strategy game where you use soldiers, robots, hovercraft, and spaceships to battle alien races.

WordPops

WordPops is an addictive cross between Tetris® and Scrabble®. Spell words with your finger to stop the bubbles from filling the screen. Earning more points by spelling difficult words.
