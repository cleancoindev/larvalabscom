Winken, Blinken and Nod

Winken, Blinken and Nod is the first iPad book that can hear you speak! Just read the story and watch the animations follow along with your voice. Read with your child and let them touch and shake the screen while the story moves along.

All the art has been created from real paper by former Martha Stewart Kids art director, Robin Rosenthal, then carefully animated with smooth, realistic motion and lots of cute effects. Winken, Blinken and Nod has become a fun part of our families’ nightly reading and we can’t wait for you to experience it with your family too.
