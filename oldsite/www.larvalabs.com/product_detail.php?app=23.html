Face Mixer v2.0

Take photos of your friends and combine their faces in ridiculous ways. Put Lisa's lips below Jerry's monobrow (sorry Jerry), then save it to your gallery so you can email to everyone you know.

Version 2.0 is a complete redesign that has all the features below plus:

  • Facebook integration! Take any photo from Facebook, mix it up with other friend's photos or ones from your camera, and upload it back to Facebook. It will automatically tagged with who's in the photo.
  • Total interface redesign
  • Includes hilarious stock photos
  • Improved performance and photo quality

And we've still got all the version 1 features too:

  • Store an unlimited number of photos in the app for lots of combinations
  • Get photo from camera or gallery
  • Drag, pinch and rotate photos to get them perfectly positioned
  • Flip through face pieces individually, or let the program make a random face for you
  • Save results to phone gallery for emailing, backgrounds, whatever
Available at the iTunes Appstore
