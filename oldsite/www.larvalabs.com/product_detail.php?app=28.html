Kick Drum Old School

Kick Drum Old School is the vintage edition of the popular drum machine that lets you write beats on your phone. It comes with five full drum kits, including two new old school sets, and a human beat box! Sounds can be played both forward and reverse, with funky results.

You can create double-length beats. Of course, the original Kick Drum features are still supported: tempo range from 50 to 300 BPM, soft and hard drum hits, and a shuffle mode, email your beat for listening and editing by your friends. And now, you can also set any beat as your ringtone!
